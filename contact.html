David was born and raised in Louisville, KY. He attended Trinity High School (’84) and University of Louisville (’89). After receiving his degree from U of L he began his long career in the outdoor advertising industry at Naegele Outdoor, which is now CBS outdoor. He also spent time as a Sales Manager at Lamar Outdoor Advertising. Then, in 1991, David founded Miller Print Group. Miller Print Group represented an in-house large format printer known as National Print Group.

In 2008 David utilized his relationships, and knowledge, from large format printing and outdoor sales to transform Miller Print Group in to a full service outdoor media agency. In 2012 MPG Media Services began its new direction as a one-stop-shop for both out of home production and outdoor space buying.

David thoroughly enjoys working alongside each and every one of his clients, and he is always focused on finding new and effective ways to utilize out-of-home to develop their brands.
Dawn co-founded Miller Print Group alongside David over 20 years ago. She has continually worked as MPG’s accountant, taking close care of all our clients, and is the head of human resources. Dawn enjoys a very active lifestyle and is a loving mother of three; but most of all, she is more than thrilled to now be a Grandmother.
Anita joined MPG in the fall of 2013. Her background in sales expands over 25 years. Prior to MPG Anita sought a diploma in Commercial Art and became a business owner at the age of just 25, which she consecutively built over a 10 year period. During this time she was offered a position which had her relocate from her Florida home to Indiana. This position led her to our President David Miller who recognized Anita’s strong sales experience and he brought her to MPG as an Account Coordinator.
Bob has been with MPG since 2008. He is a proud Veteran of the United States Marine Corp which makes him a great part of our team. Bob has over 35 years of upper management retail experience; prior to MPG he was the Executive Vice President of a major sporting goods chain. His long career in sales has shaped his work mantra which is: “Service Excellence.”
With over 25 years of graphic design experience Jamie works as our one man art department. Prior to MPG Jamie worked for National Print Group as their graphic designer for 8 years. He has been with MPG for over 15 years, designing just about every form of advertising media in the market; from product labels to billboards and massive building wraps.
Traci has been part of the MPG family from just about the beginning. She has over 30 years of experience in the outdoor industry. Traci has an impressive client list spanning multiple market areas: The Kentucky Lottery, Sun Tan City, Old National Bank, and Baptist Hospital. She is happily married to her husband Jeff, a proud mother to her two daughters and a playful Boxer, and is a loving grandmother.
Melanie joined MPG in January of 2013. She works side-by-side with David Miller as MPG’s Management Analyst to establish and improve the company’s operational procedures and to define strategic plans for revenue growth. Melanie has an impressive schooling background receiving her B.S. in Business, with a concentration in B2B Marketing and International HR, from Rennes Business School in France. She continued her education receiving her MBA from Bellarmine University in 2013. In 2014 Melanie was inducted into Beta Gamma Sigma, an honor society for business students who are regarded as “The Best in Business.” She also has a background in sales and marketing which has allowed her to coordinate and supervise some of David Miller’s accounts such as: Circle K, Planet Fitness, and Sun Tan City.
Megan joined MPG in February of 2014, after receiving a B.A. in Communication from Bellarmine University. In 2012 Megan was inducted into Lambda Pi Eta, a Communication scholar honors society, for her outstanding scholastic achievements in the Communication discipline. Before transferring to Bellarmine, Megan attended the University of Kentucky where she was the President of the Wakeboard Team, leading them to Collegiate Nationals two consecutive years, and an active member of the Women’s Lacrosse Team. She has a long history of retail sales experience which has equipped her with the skills to be a successful Account Executive at MPG. But most of all, she is a very proud Corgi mom.
Bev has worked for MPG Media since 2001 after a career of over 25 years in the legal profession. As a graduate of University of Louisville, she places her alma mater’s athletic billboard campaigns as well as provides design consultation and custom large graphics for their sports arenas. She is a trusted resource and has helped other sports organizations brand their facilities. In addition to her athletic clients, Bev places outdoor and provides custom large graphics for corporate giants like Brown Forman and Wendy’s. She has passed on her passion for advertising to her two daughters who both are also in the field, and Bev is more than pleased to take on her newest title, “Grandma.”
Carolyn has a long, impressive media buying background. She joined MPG in 2009 after retiring from 27 year long career as Brown Forman’s Outdoor Media Production Specialist. Carolyn also has handled the outdoor placement and print production for Lenox China as well as Crystal and Hartman Luggage. She is a gifted singer and is an active member of the WOC Choir in Nashville. Carolyn is happily married to her husband Walter, and is a very proud parent of her daughter.
